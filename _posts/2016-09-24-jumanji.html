Jumanji

Lions, Monkeyboys, and of course Robin Williams. They may be making a Jumanji 2 but it will never replace that classic we grew up watching over and over on VHS.

7 inches of your time

Drinking Rules

Drink Once
  • You hear the Jumanji Drums
  • Everytime they roll the dice
  • Everytime they read a rule
    • +1 if they are confused by the rule
  • When you see a Lion
  • Monkeys are up to no good, making trouble in the neighborhood
  • Someone hits a spider
  • Waterfall during stampede
