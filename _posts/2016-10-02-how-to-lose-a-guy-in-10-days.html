How to Lose a Guy in 10 Days

Whether your girlfriend is forcing you to watch or you have a soft spot for rom-coms, it doesn't matter because we can all drink to it!

Drinking Rules

Drink Once
  • Ben acts cocky or overconfident
  • Andie and/or Ben does something that makes you cringe
  • Someone on screen drinks
  • You recognize an actor who becomes famous later in their careers
Drink Twice
  • Andie attempts a "classic woman mistake"
  • Ben resists breaking with Andie
  • Andie and Ben show their real emotions towords eachother
Drink Thrice
  • Everytime they say the title of the movie
  • Someone uses the term frost/frosting
