The Simpsons: Treehouse of Horror

Halloween is upon us and so let's drink a Simpson's tradition. This drinking game is good for any Treehouse of Horror episode. You can start from season 2, the latest season or just pick a random season on whatever your favorite number is. It is always a good time!

Drinking Rules

Drink Once
  • You recognize the movie/tv show/story that is being parodied
    • If you are with a group, the first person to name the parody is exempt from +1
    • +1 if they point out a issue/loophole in the orginal stories plot
    • +1 if the parody is of a Twilight Zone episode
    • +1 You are truely impressed by well-done they parodied the orginal
  • Someone dies
  • You find yourself laughing out loud
  • During the credits, you see parody rename credit.
Drink Twice
  • Marge gives a Curtain Speech to start the show
  • Kang and/or Kodos make an appearance
    • +1 if you see a third alien, Serak the Preparer
Drink Thrice
  • Flanders turns out to be evil
