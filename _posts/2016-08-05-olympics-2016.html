Rio Olympics 2016

Athletes have been training their whole lives to complete in the Olympics. As for us not so much, so let's get drunk watching them try to achieve their dreams (and let's just be honest, most of them will fail).

Drinking Rules:

Drink Once
  • Your country wins bronze metal
  • Commercial integrates Olympics into it
  • You've never heard of a country before
  • Whenever you see an image of Muhammad Ali (RIP)
Drink Twice
  • Your country wins silver metal
  • Olympic Athelete background story about overcoming odds
  • Someone referes to Brazil's political issues, the Zika Virus or the poluted water
Drink Trice
  • Your country wins gold metal
  • You discover a sport you never knew existed
  • Someone says Michael Phelps
Drink It All
  • Your country wins all three metals; bronze, silver and gold
  • Olympic tourch is lit
  • Michael Phelps places
