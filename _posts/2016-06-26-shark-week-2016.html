Shark Week 2016

Its that time again that we spend a week learning about sharks! So grab a drink, pick and choose which rules you like below, and let's get drunking.

Drinking Rules:

Drink Once
  • Someone goes in a shark cage
  • Dramatic reenactment
  • Slowmotion of shark getting hangtime
  • When the an expereiment fails
  • Someone brags about their technology
  • "The Shallows" promo
Drink Twice
  • Voice over hypothetically tells how easily shark can kill someone
  • Anytime some does a "Shark Week Frist"
Drink Trice
  • When someone 'actually' gets in a very dangerous situation.
  • Paul de Gelder makes and apperance (former Navy diver who lost hand and leg in shark attack)
  • Dickie Chivell does something insane that risks his life
Drink It All
  • When a show ends and they have learned nothing except a new camera angle.
  • Random celebrity makes an apperience in a special
