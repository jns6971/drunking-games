The Blair Witch Project

Just because you're scared doesn't mean you don't have to drink.

Drinking Rules

Drink Once
  • Someone references the Blair Witch
  • They shake the camera so much you have no idea what is going on
  • The guys start argueing
  • Whenever your see the log or map
  • Someone is frightened bya pile of twigs or rocks
  • Anyone talks directly to the camera
  • The drone is used
  • Some one gets hurt
  • Static on walkie-talkie
  • Anytime someone is frightened
    • +1 if they scream
Drink Thrice
  • Someone sees the Blair Witch
