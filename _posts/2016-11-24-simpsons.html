The Simpsons

There are many types of marathons out there. Some like ones for running, others like ones for biking, my kind of marathon is drinking to the longest running cartoon of all time. I suggest starting season 1, episode 1 and re-live your childhood.

Drinking Rules

Drink Once
  • Homer says "Doh"
  • Someone dies
  • You find yourself laughing out loud
  • During the credits, you see parody rename credit.
Drink Twice
  • Marge gives a Curtain Speech to start the show
  • Kang and/or Kodos make an appearance
    • +1 if you see a third alien, Serak the Preparer
Drink Thrice
  • Flanders turns out to be evil
